Pie

Description

A pie is a baked dish with a pastry crust holding a sweet or savory filling, like fruit, meat, custard, or vegetables, often topped with more pastry or left open. It's defined by its crust—either a bottom crust, a top crust, or both enclosing the filling—and comes in variations like fruit pies, meat pies (steak & kidney), quiches, tarts, and creamy desserts, serving as a versatile comfort food in many cultures.

Ingredients

  • Pie crust
  • Apples
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Butter
  • Lemon juice (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Peel and slice the apples.
  3. Put the apple slices in a bowl.
  4. Add sugar, cinnamon, and lemon juice.
  5. Mix everything well.
  6. Place the apple mixture into the pie crust.
  7. Add small pieces of butter on top.
  8. Cover with the top crust if using one.
  9. Bake for 40–45 minutes until golden brown.
  10. Let it cool before serving.
