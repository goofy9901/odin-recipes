Pasta

Description

Pasta is a versatile Italian staple made from unleavened dough (usually durum wheat flour, water, and/or eggs) formed into various shapes like ribbons, tubes, or cords, then boiled or baked, serving as a base for countless dishes with sauces or fillings, categorized as dried or fresh.

Ingredients

  • 200 g pasta
  • 2-3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Oregano or Basil
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
    • Bring a pot of water to a boil
    • Add salt
    • Cook the pasta according to package instructions (8–10 minutes)
  2. Make the sauce
    • Heat olive oil in a pan
    • Add chopped garlic and cook for 30–60 seconds (don’t burn it)
    • Add tomato sauce
    • Season with salt, pepper, and herbs
    • Simmer for 5–7 minutes
  3. Combine
    • Drain the pasta
    • Add it to the sauce
    • Mix well and cook for 1–2 minutes
  4. Serve
    • Top with parmesan if you like
