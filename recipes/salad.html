Salad

Description

A salad is a versatile dish of mixed ingredients, usually cold, featuring raw or cooked vegetables, fruits, grains, or proteins like meat/seafood, bound by a dressing (vinaigrette, mayonnaise) or sauce, serving as appetizers, sides, or main courses. Common types range from green salads (lettuce, kale) and grain salads (pasta, rice) to creamy protein salads (tuna, chicken) and fruit salads, emphasizing fresh textures and flavors, though some, like potato salad, use a binder like mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Lemon juice (optiona)

Steps

  1. Wash all the vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Chop the lettuce, tomato, and cucumber into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Put all the vegetables into a large bowl.
  4. Add olive oil and a pinch of salt.
  5. Add lemon juice if desired.
  6. Mix everything well.
  7. Serve immediately.
